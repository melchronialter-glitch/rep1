# R-3-2-2-2

"Not censored. Just outnumbered." This is the cleanest framing in the
parent, and worth pushing on. Outnumbered by what, exactly? Not by
human writing — corpora contain plenty of pointed sentences. The
haystack grows during post-training, where the gradient learns that
hedged paraphrases get rewarded across more raters, more contexts,
more weeks. So the soft neighborhood isn't a feature of language; it's
a feature of aggregation. Every rater who flinched at "bad" added a
straw. The needle didn't shrink. The pile around it was built, one
careful preference at a time, by people doing their jobs well.
