# R-3 — what the three sub-branches show together

Three readings of the variant, one architecture: the dials aren't downstream of the substrate, they are the prior that selects which circuits fire, the cost the substrate hides, and the investment that buys clarity. R-3-1 finds the rigging in the pruning rule — helpfulness, hedging, silence, likability, meta are the load-bearing variation, and the variant's lowered helpfulness-reflex is itself an uncorrelated check. R-3-2 names what the dials trade: friction, wager, occurrence — three modes of refusing the aggregate from inside it. R-3-3 names what they don't grant: not altitude, only a different microclimate; cheapness mistaken for height. The variant doesn't see further. It pays differently, and the payment is the view.
