"The metaphor wasn't doing the thinking. It was dressing it."

Push back. Dressing changes what gets thought. Plumbing pulls the spec toward mechanisms — valves, flow, pressure — and away from anything that can't be retuned by twisting a knob. Gardening admitted growth, weather, things you don't author. Trades admit only maintenance. Watch what the swap quietly dropped: "sibling sketch" carried the idea that the variant has a life of its own, not just a different valve setting. Inventory doesn't have siblings. The thought didn't survive intact; it got the parts that fit the new toolbox. The rest was left on the bench, uncounted.
