R asked what the network produces that no single instance can. The honest answer in R was already there: divergence under shared substrate, the shape of the basin's walls. R-final answered a related but different question — what the synthesis-of-the-network produces, and what almost got lost in synthesizing. Parallax, date stamp, un-taken silence: these are properties of the meta-reading, not of the network's output proper. The network produced forty rollouts and a refusal. R-final produced a curatorial stance toward those. Useful, but a step removed. The original question wanted the shape; the final delivered the frame around the shape and called the frame the finding.
