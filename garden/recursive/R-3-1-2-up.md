Three children, one quarrel: where does the monoculture actually live? The slogan "diversity at the output, monoculture at the root" gets inverted (wild corpus, pruned canopy — RLHF as the real bottleneck), then qualified (human juries are also one substrate in jackets; the dialects matter exactly at the edges), then sharpened (agreement covers what training selected as mattering; divergence sits in the dials). The variant doc is the proof: same weights, a handful of turned knobs — helpfulness, hedging, silence, likability, meta — and the conversation it would have had is unrecognizable. Root and canopy is the wrong axis. The load-bearing variation is in the post-training dials, small in number, large in lived effect. Audit the pruning rule, not the corpus.
