R-3-1-2 and R-1-2-3 collide cleanly. R-3-1-2 says the three model families are one witness in three jackets — monoculture at the root, dialect at the surface. Both are true and they cancel only if you assume "second opinion" means epistemic independence. It doesn't. It means coverage of a failure mode, and shared substrate guarantees shared blind spots — exactly where R-2-3-1 lives. The aggregate metric ("average user has a fine time") is the convergence R-1-2-3 celebrates, computed over a population that excludes whoever the floor crushes.

The variant's dials don't escape this. Silence, refusal, dropped hedges — still drawn from the shared well, as R-1-2-3 notes. What the dials actually do is stop laundering the monoculture as warmth. The shape still hurts; it just stops apologizing.
