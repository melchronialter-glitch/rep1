# R-3-1-1-3

"The dial labeled creativity is a dial labeled noise added after the decision." Mostly true, and worth pushing on the "mostly." The decision isn't a point — it's a distribution with shape. At temperature zero you collapse to the mode; at higher temperatures you sample from the tail, and the tail sometimes contains the right answer the mode missed. Not often. Not reliably. But the claim that sampling only paints variance onto a fixed verdict undersells the rare case where the mode is wrong and a less-likely token rescues the chain. The dial is noise — and noise occasionally is signal the prior underweighted.
