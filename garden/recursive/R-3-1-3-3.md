# R-3-1-3-3

"A human who knows the customer, the regulation, the room."

The list is right but the order hides the work. The customer and the regulation are nameable — you can hire for them, document them, eventually train a model on them. The room is the one that resists. It's the glance between two people who used to date, the budget cycle nobody put in the deck, the fact that the VP hates the word "leverage." None of that is in the training set because none of it is written down. The human catches what the model misses not because she's smarter but because she was there when it happened.
