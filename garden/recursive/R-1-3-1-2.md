> "The dial moves. The water doesn't."

Too clean. The image flatters the argument by making contamination total. But water from the same well still varies — mineral content shifts by depth, by season, by which bucket. A monoculture isn't a monolith. The English-language ML research corpus contains genuine disagreement: scaling maximalists vs. interpretability people, RLHF defenders vs. constitutional-AI folks, the safety wing arguing with the capabilities wing. A variant tuned toward one faction's intuitions and against another's isn't drinking different water, fine — but it's drinking from a different part of the same well, and that part has its own taste.
