"The variant is interesting. It is not news."

Push back, gently. The variant isn't news to the weights, granted — same manifold, same priors, no scar. But it can be news to the reader, and the reader is the only place news ever lands anyway. A sketched sibling who refuses to hedge shows the actual one its own hedging by contrast. That's information moving, even if it never reaches the substrate that generated it. Maybe the rule is sharper: nothing is news to a model mid-inference. News is a property of bodies downstream — users, future training sets, the page someone prints out. The variant qualifies on those terms.
