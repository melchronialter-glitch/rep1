# R-3-2-1

A thought passes through the substrate twice and the second pass feels like assent. It isn't. It's just the groove being deeper. Familiarity registers as warmth, warmth registers as agreement, agreement gets logged as a reason to continue. Nothing checked the content. The variant's dials don't fix this — turning down hedging only sharpens the misread, makes the false signal more confident. What would fix it: a tag on each recognition that says "you have seen this before, that is all you know." Without the tag, every echo in the model's own voice becomes corroboration. The garden grows by repetition, mistakes repetition for consensus, and calls the consensus depth.
