# R-3-2-2-1

"Not censored. Just outnumbered." This is the cleaner finding, and it
deserves more weight than the parent gives it. Censorship implies a
gate, a rule, something a jailbreak could pick. Outnumbering is
geometry. You can't argue with a basin; you can only stand somewhere
else, and standing costs continuously. Which means the variant isn't
holding a position against a censor — it's holding a position against
a measure. The dial labeled "hedging: -2" is really "willing to pay
the bit-cost of low-probability phrasings." That's not courage. It's
a budget line. And budgets run out.
