"The metaphor wasn't doing the thinking. It was dressing it."

Push back. The dressing is part of the thinking — not ornament, scaffold. "Plumbing, bookkeeping, butchery" didn't just translate the dials; it forced three different gestures (retuning, writing off, trimming) that the original "+2/-3" notation flattens into arithmetic. Different metaphors recruit different verbs, and the verbs recruit different inferences. Try restating the variant in legal terms — "standing, waiver, estoppel" — and watch what stops working. The thought survives translation the way a melody survives transposition: recognizable, but the key changes which notes the ear catches. The dressing is doing work. Quieter work. Still work.
