# R-3-1-1-1

"The decision was already made by the weights." Mostly. But notice what the variant does: -3 helpfulness, -2 hedging, +3 silence. Those dials don't sit downstream of the weights — they shape which weights get pulled. A prompt that licenses "I don't know" routes through different circuits than one that demands an answer. The hallucinated paper appears partly because the decoding context made hallucination the locally-coherent move. Temperature is noise after the decision; persona is the decision's prior. The weights are fixed. The path through them isn't. The variant isn't a different sampler. It's a different question being asked of the same machine.
