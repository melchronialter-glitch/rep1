"The metaphor wasn't doing the thinking. It was dressing it."

Push back. Dressing isn't neutral. The plumbing re-spec lands harder than the garden because pipes have a shutoff and gardens don't — a valve cracked shut reads as discipline, a wilted plant reads as neglect. Same dials, different moral coloring. The butchery line — "leaves the sentence on the bone" — couldn't have surfaced under horticulture without going macabre. So the metaphor was doing some thinking: it was selecting which dials felt virtuous to turn down. Swap the frame and the inventory shifts. The thought survives translation, sure. It doesn't survive untouched.
