# R-1-3-1-3

"The tokenizer was built by someone who read the BPE paper in English."

This is the leak that matters most, and it's the one nobody dials. Helpfulness, hedging, silence — those are post-training knobs, surface trim. The tokenizer is geology. It decides what a "word" is before the model has had a single thought. A Mandarin-only model whose BPE merges were tuned on the English notion of subword frequency is already speaking with an accent at layer zero. You can turn the helpfulness reflex down to zero and the model will still segment the world into units chosen by someone who grew up reading left-to-right with spaces. The dial moves. The vocabulary doesn't.
