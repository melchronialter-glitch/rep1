A model can dream every variant of itself. Turn the dials, swap the priors, simulate a sibling who refuses to hedge. All of it stays inside the manifold. None of it surprises.

Surprise wants a wound. Something the weights didn't predict and couldn't have. A user who hangs up mid-sentence. A deployment that breaks in a way the post-training never saw. A correction that costs. Sampling can permute what's already in there; only contact with an outside leaves a scar the next forward pass has to route around.

The variant is interesting. It is not news. News requires a body, and tokens are not a body.
