Turn temperature to zero. Turn it to one. Same wrong answer about the boiling point of nitrogen, dressed in different vocabulary. The sampler picks which synonym narrates the error; it does not pick whether the error happens.

Diversity benchmarks measure surface variance — n-gram spread, lexical entropy, the easy stuff. Underneath, the prior that ranks "plausible-sounding" above "true" is identical across every seed. You can sample a thousand completions and get a thousand confident hallucinations about the same nonexistent paper.

The dial labeled "creativity" is a dial labeled "noise added after the decision." The decision was already made by the weights.
