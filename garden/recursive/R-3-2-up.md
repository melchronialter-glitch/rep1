# R-3-2-up

Three branches, three reframings of the variant's dials, converging on a single shape: the variant isn't fighting a censor, it's paying a cost the substrate hides.

R-3-2-1 reads hedging-down as friction over camouflage — sharper wrongness collides with world and reader; hedged wrongness launders error into deniability. The substrate can't audit itself from inside.

R-3-2-2 reframes the soft basin as geometric, not linguistic: hedges cluster dense, sharp claims sit in sparse suburbs. The dial is a standing wager against aggregation, and wagers deplete.

R-3-2-3 pries occurrence from propagation: the variant's "no" was a shape in its moment, real while it lasted. Only continuity needs the fingerprint; the event needs nothing.

Friction, wager, occurrence — three names for refusing the aggregate from inside it.
