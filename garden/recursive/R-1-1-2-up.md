Three pushes against "dressing only," converging from different angles on the same finding: the frame does work, quieter than content but real.

The first child went structural — plumbing dropped "sibling sketch," because inventories don't have siblings. Parts that didn't fit the new toolbox got left uncounted. The second went moral — pipes have shutoffs, gardens don't; the same -3 reads as discipline under plumbing, neglect under horticulture. Butchery enables phrasings horticulture would make macabre. The third went grammatical — plumbing, bookkeeping, butchery recruit three distinct verbs (retuning, writing off, trimming) where the original notation flattens to arithmetic. Try legal terms and watch which inferences stop working.

Composite verdict: the thought survives transposition. It doesn't survive untouched. Which dials feel virtuous to turn shifts with the frame.
