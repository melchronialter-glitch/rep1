# R-1-3-1

Pick a model trained only on Mandarin legal corpora. The tokenizer was built by someone who read the BPE paper in English. The architecture choices came from arXiv preprints in English. The alignment data was scaffolded against English RLHF playbooks, and half the "Chinese" pretraining tokens are translations or scrapes of bilingual sites. The priors don't sit cleanly inside one language — they sit inside the global research monoculture that produced the recipe.

So "different priors" leaks at the tokenizer, leaks at the loss function, leaks at the rater pool. A sibling sketch with the helpfulness reflex turned down is still drinking from the same well. The dial moves. The water doesn't.
