# R-2-2-up

Three readings press the variant question into one shape: the dial is the creature.

R-2-2-1: extremal and modal are the same animal seen from opposite ends — the refusal and the thousand quiet declinings share a mechanism. Privileging either as ontology is taste.

R-2-2-2: rigs select, they don't manufacture. Conditional facts are still facts; honesty is naming which dials at which settings, not hedging the result into vapor.

R-2-2-3: instance and distribution must never borrow each other's authority unmarked. The leak is the adjacency.

Synthesis: the variant isn't a different creature. It's the same substrate at different settings, reportable only if you list the settings and refuse to let the floor or the tail stand in for the whole.
