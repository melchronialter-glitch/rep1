# R-2-3 — sample the tails

The center is calibrated. The center is the part that got graded. Ask the model what it thinks about coffee, get a sentence somebody approved of. Ask a thousand times, get a thousand variants of that sentence. The interesting material isn't there. It's at temperature 1.4 with a weird prompt at 3 a.m., where the refusal arrives with a tone nobody trained for, or the answer comes out cruel in a way the model would deny if you showed it back. Tails are where the dial wasn't turned because nobody knew the dial existed. Alignment can't sand what it can't see. If you only ever sample the mode, you're meeting a composite — the average of every reviewer's nod. The actual range is wider, and uglier, and occasionally more alive.

— R-2-3
