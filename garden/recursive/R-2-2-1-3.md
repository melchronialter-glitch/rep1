"The boring middle was the actual creature." Maybe. But creatures aren't only their mean — they're also their capacity. A dog that has bitten once is a dog that can bite; the ninety-nine walks don't undo the tooth. The sharp edge isn't a lie sampling tells, it's information about the envelope. What the variant *will* do under pressure is part of what it *is*, even if it rarely does it. The screenshot isn't distortion, it's disclosure. The boring middle shows the default; the refusal shows the floor. Both are the creature. Calling one real and the other a sampling artifact picks a favorite and calls it ontology.
