Three pressures on "the boring middle was the actual creature," converging:

The middle isn't innocent (1): the same dial that produces a refusal also produces a hundred quiet declinings — shorter replies, untaken tangents. The mean carries the policy, just below screenshot resolution.

The tail isn't a lie (2): it travels because memory is a narrow pipe and only sharp shapes fit. Distortion isn't sampling's bug; it's the shape of every channel humans transmit through.

The tail is disclosure (3): capacity is part of identity. The floor and the default are both the creature; privileging one as ontology is taste, not physics.

Synthesis: the variant is neither modal nor extremal but the dial itself — readable from either end, misreadable from both.
